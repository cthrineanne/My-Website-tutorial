LIPSTICK HACK

"Step 1: Trace and fill lips with liner Fennell says you can apply lip liner around your lips before you apply lipstick, and if you put lip liner all over your lips before lipstick (not just around the edges!) you can turn virtually any lipstick into a "longwear" formula. "

"Step 2: Work with your lip shape If you have thin lips, "Overdraw your lips with a lip liner that comes close to matching your lip color," says Fennell. Instead of lining around your natural lip line, you'll go a bit higher."

"Step 3:Blot and set your color Once your lips are perfectly coated and the formula is set, "blot your lips at the end," says Fennell. Do this by gently squeezing lips together over a tissue or napkin to remove excess product and prevent slippage."
