EYE MAKEUP TUTORIAL

"Step 1: Use an eye prime-We insist that you always use an eye primer! After all, that’s precisely what will make your eye makeup stay put for a longer time. Eye primer helps create a smooth surface for the makeup to easily glide on. "

"Step 2: Choose basic shades-It’s important to first start your eye makeup with the basic shades, you can always go crazy with colors and glitters once you’ve got a hold of it.."

"Step 3: Choose your blending tools-Eye makeup brushes are different from the other makeup brushes, though there are beauty artists out there who just use their fingertips for eye makeup too! We suggest you use a brush if you are at a beginner level."
