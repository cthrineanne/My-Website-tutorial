EYE BROW TUTORIAL

"Eyebrow Threading: Threading is generally considered to be one of the safest methods of brow grooming. It's especially suitable for those who struggle with folliculitis or post-wax breakouts. "

"Find your ideal brow. The inner corner of your brows should be in line with the sides of your nose. You can use a long pencil, your make-up brush, or some other object to measure this."

"Use tweezers to pluck unwanted hairs. Pluck any hairs that you find outside of your ideal brow line. Find the root of the hair with the prongs of the tweezers and pull quickly in the opposite direction of the hair growth to remove the hairs completely."
